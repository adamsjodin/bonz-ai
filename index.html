During the turn of the 19th century, Bergstorg was once the meeting grounds for Stockholms high society and cultural elite. With the foundation of Bonz.ai, the square on which it stands has regained its former prominence. Bonz.ai is a destination where contemporary art, fine dining, and exquisite interior design meet to create a modern luxury hotel in central Stockholm.
